Best Cities for a Relaxing Winter Visit to Europe

Written by Khushi Desar on December 21, 2023

While our research maintains editorial independence, we have the potential to receive a commission for any purchases made through the links we provide.

Europe is a continent of great diversity, a smorgasbord of experiences that will leave you with a suitcase full of beautiful memories and your gallery bursting with Instagram-worthy moments. You can start your day here in Paris with the elegance of the Eiffel Tower, and waltz into Vienna to indulge in classical music as well as grand palaces & amazing architecture, and cozy coffeehouses. Jump over to Switzerland next where precision meets chocolate and every bar is a masterpiece. And if you are feeling festive, you can crash in Germany to celebrate the great Oktoberfest or fly to Finland to capture the majestic Northern Lights. Last but not least, end your day in the comfort of gorgeous Scottish castles surrounded by mist and water, making your fairytale dream finally come true. 

Venice, Italy

Start your day here in the city of canals, Venice set in northeastern Italy, and be mesmerized by its beauty and charm. You will come across a number of charming bridges, intricate networks of canals, and historic architecture that will truly capture your heart and soul. Romantic getaway or family retreat, Venice has its options ready for you. Couples can take a romantic ride along the narrow canals which are guided by gondoliers, or you can simply visit the nearest architectural heritage sites with your family. St. Mark’s Basilica and the Doge’s Palace are two highlights of the place that you must visit along with the Biennale di Venezia.

Vienna, is a city in Austria that not only seamlessly blends in with the rich historical past but also embraces contemporary culture. Interlaken, Switzerland

Interlaken is a perfect place if you are looking to spend a night in the core of the Swiss Alps, between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz. It is a stunning destination, often referred to as a picturesque resort town boasting alpine landscapes, charming architecture, and plenty of outdoorsy activities perfect for those seeking adventurous trips. It is surrounded by gorgeous landmarks like Eiger, Monch, and Jungfrau mountains alongside a range of recreational activities to choose from, from paragliding to bungee jumping this place is heaven for adventure enthusiasts. The best part about Interlaken is its festivals and events that are hosted throughout the year so don’t miss out on that.

Rovaniemi, Finland

 The capital of Lapland, Rovaniemi is a beautiful city located in northern Finland. The city is internationally known as the official hometown of Santa Claus boasting a festive atmosphere and Christmas-themed activities, and now has become a very loved destination for those looking for a unique Arctic retreat because of its Arctic landscape. The place also offers great outdoor activities such as husky sledging, snowmobiling, and reindeer safaris. And during winter, you can witness the majestic northern lights across the sky. This place is a must-visit destination.
