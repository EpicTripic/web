Rome is an evergreen city where history and modernity collide creating a beautiful tapestry combining everything from rich cuisine to Romanian culture. The city will keep you hooked and captivated by its beauty. And then there’s food- what better place than here for those who love carbs? The place is full of pasta and more pasta, pizza, and tiramisu galore. An absolute delight for a food lover! Also, Rome preaches a popular saying ‘Dolce Vita’ meaning, the sweetness of doing nothing. And it's not every day you get to do nothing but here in Rome, you sip on wine and embrace the timeless charm and chaos of the city.
