Amsterdam, a gorgeous city that never misses to catch attention with its rich historic significance, beautiful canals, and vintage charm, also referred to as the Venice of the North is located in the heart of the Netherlands. And can’t miss the fact that the city owns more bikes than there are people, everywhere you go you’ll see bikes. Don’t be surprised if you see pancakes everywhere too. Pancakes are the delight of Amsterdam, beautifully powdered sugar, caramelized apples, and cheese, what could go wrong?
